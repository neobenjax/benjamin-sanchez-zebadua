## The Mission
The CN Cycle for CHEO brings together the Ottawa community to fund critical cancer research and care for children. The sheer scale of the event demands a massive, coordinated volunteer effort to manage site infrastructure before the athletes arrive.

## The Role
Serving in **General Support**, my focus was entirely on operational efficiency and site readiness:
- **Site Integrity:** Assisting with the assembly and teardown of event structures.
- **Athlete Logistics:** Directing early-arriving participants, answering queries, and ensuring flow control at registration choke points.

## Architect's Lessons
**Leadership through Involvement:** Before you can architect a system, you must understand its components. This role was an exercise in pure execution and observation. Working directly with the public and fellow volunteers taught me that even the smallest logistical failure can cascade into major bottlenecks. 

By taking ownership of unglamorous tasks and maintaining a positive, solution-oriented mindset, I helped ensure the event's underlying infrastructure held strong, allowing the participants to focus entirely on the cause.
