# The Death of Portfolio Procrastination

For years, developers have procrastinated on their personal brands. We get stuck in the "choice paralysis" of frameworks, CSS configurations, and deployment pipelines. 

By applying **VibeCoding**—a philosophy of high-level orchestration over low-level syntax—I built my **FinTech Architect** platform from scratch to production in under four hours.
